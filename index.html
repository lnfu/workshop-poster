BioArt workshop #1 _The art of SynBio_ Bacteria paintings

Join the lab to make your own BioArt with the sparkling E. coli and explore the science behind synthetic biology. No biology experience is required apart from curiosity and a creative spirit!

About this event

Synthetic biology is an innovative field of science that involves redesigning organisms for specific purposes by engineering them with new abilities. The power of synthetic biology has been used to solve many problems in biomedicine, agriculture and manufacturing, and beyond. From vaccines to boost immunity, bioreactor to degrade PET, to fabric made from spider silk, synthetic biology has been collaborate with humans in a wide range. As exciting as the advance of these innovation technologies are, it often seems hard for us to engage with scientific world . So, how do we begin to understand synbio that has changed our life in many aspect? How do we engage with this cool innovations?

In this workshop, you will:

  • Learn the basic knowledge and application of synthetic biology
  • Consider the ethical implications of technology in synthetic biology
  • Make your own art piece by using your choosing E. coli expressing different fluorescent protein
